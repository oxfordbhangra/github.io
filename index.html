About Us

Oxford Bhangra is a university-wide bhangra society encompassing all colleges at Oxford University and Oxford Brookes. Oxford Bhangra hosts weekly classes which are open for anyone to attend, regardless of ability! The team performs across Oxford at many balls, cultural events and other occasions..

Bhangra is a fun and energetic dance from Punjab, India. It's all about high energy, colorful outfits, and awesome music. Originally, it was danced to celebrate the harvest, but now it's got a modern twist with performances incorporating pop songs and cool moves. It's not just a dance; it's a way to bring people together, showing off how cool and welcoming Punjabi culture is. Imagine dancing with friends, laughing, and enjoying the beat. Bhangra is exactly that - a blast of fun, colors, and tunes that everyone can enjoy. It's a great way to dive into a culture that's all about having a good time and sharing it with others.

If you have ever wanted to be a part of Zumba, Bhangra, or appreciate South Asian performance art, this is for you!
